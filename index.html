Bridging the Digital Literacy Gap

Millions of students worldwide face a digital literacy gap, struggling with language barriers, limited guidance, and a lack of personalized support. Existing educational tools are often too generic, inaccessible, or fail to adapt to a student's specific learning materials, leaving them to struggle with complex concepts and information overload.

Meet EduVault: Your Personal AI Tutor

EduVault is an AI-powered personalized learning assistant designed to transform static educational content into a dynamic, interactive experience.

Core Features

Generates Q&A and Summaries

Converts content into digestible summaries and answers user questions.

Explains Complex Concepts

Simplifies difficult topics using analogies and examples tailored to the user's grade level.

Boosts Retention

Automatically creates mini-quizzes and flashcards to reinforce learning.

Ensures Accessibility

Supports multilingual input and output, making quality education globally accessible.
